Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join S4DS?

Simply fill out our contact form or attend one of our events. We welcome students from all engineering disciplines who are passionate about data science.

Are there any membership fees?

No, S4DS membership is completely free for all TCET students. We believe in making data science education accessible to everyone.

Do I need prior programming experience?

Not at all! We have programs for beginners as well as advanced learners. Our workshops start from the basics and gradually progress to advanced topics.

How often do you organize events?

We organize events regularly - typically 2-3 workshops per month, plus seminars, competitions, and guest lectures throughout the academic year.
