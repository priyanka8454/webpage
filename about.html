About S4DS

We are a student-driven organization dedicated to fostering data science education and creating opportunities for students to excel in the field of data analytics and machine learning.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by providing practical data science education and real-world project experience.

Our Vision

To create a community of skilled data scientists who can contribute meaningfully to technological advancement and societal development.

Our Values

Collaboration, innovation, continuous learning, and ethical application of data science principles in solving real-world problems.

What Makes Us Special

Student-Led Initiative

Completely managed and operated by passionate students from various engineering disciplines.

Hands-On Learning

Focus on practical implementation rather than just theoretical knowledge.

Industry Connections

Regular interactions with industry professionals and real-world project opportunities.

Recognition & Growth

Opportunities to showcase skills through competitions and collaborative projects.

Our Journey

Founded by passionate students at Thakur College of Engineering & Technology, S4DS has grown from a small study group to a thriving community of data science enthusiasts. We believe in learning by doing and have successfully organized numerous workshops, hackathons, and industry interaction sessions.

Our members have gone on to secure positions at top tech companies and pursue advanced studies in data science and machine learning. Join us to be part of this exciting journey!
